welcome to as-sua'dah foundation

As-sua'dah Foundation is a platform formed by a group of youths in Islam wiilling to pull their intelectual and financial resources together to enhance other youths.

Religious enlightment

We give lectures about the religion of Islam and it's teachings from time to time.

Values imbibing

We also teach the values of Islam and it's ethics so that the youths can be good ambassador of Islam.

Training other youths

We are also willing to pull resources together to engage other youths in skills acquisition and enhance success

Some problems were discovered among the Muslims youths which is the reason why we are set up to address these issues and shape the youths to become good representatives of Islam.
